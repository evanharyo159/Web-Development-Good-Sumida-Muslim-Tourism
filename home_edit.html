Sumida City is located in the eastern part of Tokyo and is a lively area rich in traditional downtown charm. Centered around the iconic Tokyo Skytree, the district offers plenty of opportunities for sightseeing and shopping. In Ryogoku, known as the heart of sumo wrestling, you can visit famous landmarks like the Ryogoku Kokugikan (Sumo Arena) and the Edo-Tokyo Museum. Along the Sumida River, the cherry blossoms and fireworks festivals are cherished seasonal traditions. Sumida City beautifully combines history and modernity, making it a fascinating area to explore.
